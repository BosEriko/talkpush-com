Features

 

Talkpush is changing the recruitment world thanks to its unique and disrupting features. Our product team is mainly focused on one thing: making it easier for candidates to get heard. We are excited to work with companies that want to improve the candidate experience by making the job application process faster, easier and more optimized for mobile, all the while giving candidates a chance to tell you who they are.

Our software is integrated with the leading cloud communications platform and the leading voice transcription engine, making it easy to connect with candidates from all over the world, in over 30 languages. In fact, Talkpush is the only universal platform which can engage with every candidate, regardless of what phone they have or how connected or technology-savvy they are.

To expand your talent pool further, our software collects applications from all channels, including social media and messaging platforms. In order to get real-time engagement from candidates, we have even built the first recruitment Chatbot which can initiate interview phone calls automatically. This is just one of the many tools we have built in order to turn your social media presence into a talent sourcing engine. Once you’ve let the talent talk, we bet you will be find many hidden gems in your talent pool.

Interview questionnaire

Design that perfect interview experience by choosing from 300+ pre-recorded interview questions in 4 different languages. You can also personalize and record your own interview questions.

Résumé Parsing

Any imported résumé is automatically parsed, allowing you to organize your candidates data by location, years of experience of keywords.

Social media sourcing

Engage and qualify candidates on social media at a fraction of the cost it would cost you on traditional job boards. Talkpush effectively builds candidate profiles without the need for a resume, which is ideal for sourcing on talent on social media, where most of the traffic is mobile. Read on here.

Optimize operations with analytics

Real time data is available for recruiters and their managers to measure everything from the quality of each sourcing channel, to the activity level of each recruiter, to the overall performance of the recruitment effort.

Video interview

Using the phone application (available on Google Play store for Android), you can ask all your candidates to attach a short video message with their application. The video is short and low on bandwidth consumption to guarantee maximum completion rate.

Google Integration and Transcripts

Talkpush is fully integrated with Google, with the ability to import candidates from Google Docs and to extract the interview transcript from Google Speech, available in over 30 different languages.

SMS Management

Send individual or mass SMS to all your candidates. All the SMS will be branded (with your company name as the sender) and personalized (so each SMS is addressed to the individual recipient) automatically to guarantee the maximum open rate.

Simplified application forms

Ask candidates to send in their application by sending an SMS or by visiting a dedicated career page in order to capture as many applications as possible from your recruitment advertising efforts via billboard, radio or TV.
