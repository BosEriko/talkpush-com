Software Engineer

Full time, Hong Kong

The Software Engineer will hold the following responsibilities:

- Develop state-of-the-art web applications for business users
- Collaborate with the senior team (System Architect and Senior Programmer) on application architecture design and decision-making
- Develop and test for cross-browser and device compatibility
- Troubleshoot production support issues post release deployment and come up with solutions
- Develop pioneering approaches to deliver cloud-based software services

Qualifications:

- Bachelor degree in computer science or equivalent experience
- Experience in software programming
- Experience using JavaScript resources such as jQuery
- Good level of written and spoken English
- Experience using Ruby on Rails to develop web applications is welcome but not required
- Experience in web UI development with AJAX, HTML5, CSS3, JSON, XML is welcome but not required

 
 

Account manager

Full time, Philippines

As part of the core team of this exciting startup, the Account Manager will play a central role in helping the company grow, by building the building the business with existing clients, reaching out to new ones, and helping newcomers get the most from their software.

As a member of both the business development and the operations team, the Account Manager will work in a challenging, fast-paced environment, where he/she will be asked to talk to prospective customers, train new customers, and configure their system to improve the customer experience.

The Account Manager will hold the following responsibilities:

- Identify potential customers, particularly in the Philippines
- Meet with HR departments of some of the largest organizations to help them automate some of their recruitment process using Talkpush
- Discuss requirements with prospective customer and set up their trial accounts to help convert them into paying customers
- Generate regular reports to measure user adoption and propose system improvements to increase utilization

Qualifications:

Required:

- Experienced, disciplined, and organized sales and account management professional selling to large size businesses.
- Minimum 5 years of prior work experience preferably selling professional services to companies
- Bachelor degree for a top university
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, fully fluent in English
- Able to work autonomously in a fast-paced startup environment

Preferred but not mandatory:

- Prior experience in recruitment and/or human resources
- Prior experience in consulting and IT
- Prior experience working abroad

 
 

DIGITAL HR MARKETING ANALYST

Full time, Philippines

As part of the Client Services team, the Digital HR Marketing Analyst will help to promote exciting career opportunities across multiple career sites, social media sites, and other relevant media properties.

Working alongside the Account Executives to accelerate the employer's ability to connect with the right talent, the Marketing Analyst will need creativity to build differentiated campaigns, good communications skills to work with client teams, and good analytical skills to be able to report on the performance metrics of each campaign.

The Marketing Analyst will hold the following responsibilities:

- Work with the web design team to create great career sites
- Research innovations in online employer branding and share best practices
- Responsible for employer advertising campaigns across various online channels
- Quantitative and qualitative research on the topics of employer branding and online talent communication
- Analysis of the gathered data and creation of trends and insights
- Generate regular reports to measure user adoption and propose system improvements to increase utilization

Qualifications:

Required:
- Excellent written and oral communications, experience writing copy in a professional environment
- Bachelor degree for a top university
- Comfortable working with designers, developers and with the latest technologies
- Knowledgeable on social media marketing best practices
- Able to work autonomously in a fast-paced startup environment

Preferred but not mandatory:
- Prior experience in advertising or publishing
- Prior experience in web design
