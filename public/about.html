About Talkpush

Talkpush is a technology solution provider specialized in accelerating talent acquisition through the automation of the sourcing and screening of candidates. The company's core product is a platform which allows to capture and assess the voice of thousands of job seekers on a daily basis. Founded in Hong Kong in 2013, Talkpush has rapidly expanded its operations and now supports employers in over 10 countries.

In an age when everyone is connected via their mobile phones, it is still surprisingly difficult for job candidates to get their voices heard by prospective employers. The Talkpush team started with a simple goal in mind: make it as easy as possible for employers to hear what every candidate has to say.
