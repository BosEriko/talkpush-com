Augmented Recruiter

Every month, we feature recruiters who embrace automation in our Augmented Recruiters Series.

For October 2017, we feature Angeni Kirsten Miranda from Alorica

7 Questions to Angeni Kirsten Miranda

Hello please tell us about your scope of responsibilities at Alorica?

I am the onsite coordinator for Alorica Center (MJ Plaza). I handle 3 people of 3 different channels. I monitor their productivity and ensure that they hit their targets on a daily basis. I help in managing Talkpush leads and do callouts as well. I am also responsible with the daily and weekly reports and lastly, I do training visits and conduct talks to promote our ERP (Employee Referral Program) channel.

What do you like most working in recruitment?

There are a lot of things that I enjoy doing in recruitment. One is, meeting different kinds of people every day, because I get to know their personalities, their life stories and experiences (partly) and learn from what they share. Another is, of course, fulfilling the ramps and hitting the targets daily. And, It may sound crazy but I find it fulfilling when we see the applicants and employees happy knowing that we’ve served them well. In short, giving the best customer service every single day.

How has technology changed the way you work since you started out in recruitment?

It changed a lot. Everything became easier now because of technology. I remember getting leads manually, doing callouts manually and at the end of the day sending them SMS messages. Wow! It was really time consuming (and tiring!). Can’t imagine going back to that process. And the best change is, it saves me time! Less OTs for me!

What is the future role of Artificial Intelligence in recruitment?

I would say that Artificial Intelligence brings convenience not only in recruitment, but also to the applicants.

What makes the recruitment process you have built different/unique?

For me, what makes us different is, we use Artificial Intelligence. In just few clicks you can attract more applicants without much effort calling and texting them manually since you can do all those in the Talkpush platform. Plus, the artificial intelligence platform is interactive which makes the applicants more engaged and interested in applying. Imagine that you can upload your video when you apply. Amazing, right?

What new hack/tool/technology do you recommend to your fellow “augmented recruiters?”

I would highly recommend using the Talkpush platform. Artificial Intelligence is the new thing in recruiting! It doesn’t just make your work life easier, it also helps you to be more productive and efficient recruiter. Talkpush has everything that you need in recruiting. You just need to spread the word and see how the people engage into this awesome technology.

Enough about recruitment! What is your favourite science fiction movie of all time?

STAR WARS, of course!

About Talkpush
