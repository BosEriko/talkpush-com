Augmented Recruiter

Every month, we feature recruiters who embrace automation in our Augmented Recruiters Series.

For November 2017, we feature Ram Jakaria from [24]7

7 Questions to Ram Jakaria

Hello please tell us about your scope of work responsibilities?
I’m Ram and works as a Sourcing Specialist of [24]7 Customer Philippines.  I’m part of the Sourcing team and I screen applicants over the phone to validate if they have met the requirements needed for the job and I then invite them to our recruitment site to continue the recruitment process.
What do you like most working in recruitment?
It’s both exciting and rewarding. I get the opportunity to change someone’s life for the better which is a great feeling. I can play a massive part in making it happen.
How has technology changed the way you work since you started out in recruitment?
I have been part of the Sourcing team for the past 14 months and I have seen how technology converted our recruitment process from being manual to digital which means we could now do almost everything with just one click. We are no longer restricted to doing recruitment within a certain time and space, since we integrated social media in our sourcing, which means we’re essentially hiring 24/7.
What is the future role of Artificial Intelligence in recruitment?
I think AI in recruitment saves an awful lot of time, provides better candidate experiences, increases recruiter productivity and finally creates a great first impression with candidates, which reinforces our employer brand.
What makes the recruitment process you have built different/unique?
We invest on our candidate recruitment experience to attract high caliber applicants. Word of mouth can be very influential. We increase that by making sure we treat every candidate with equal respect regardless of whether they fit for the job or not. It is important to acknowledge their effort and the investment in time they made to apply to our company.
Aside from Talkpush what are your other favourite tools/ platforms that you use will you recommend to your fellow augmented recruiters?
Facebook!
Enough about recruitment! What is your favourite science fiction movie of all time?
I think ‘The Matrix’ is the best sci-fi movie of all time. Keanu Reeves has been my favorite Hollywood actor ever since. This movie is heavy on special effects which I enjoy so much. Not to mention the brooding paranoia which is mind boggling!

About Talkpush

Talkpush is making volume hiring fun and personal again. Talkpush is a recruitment platform that leverages the power of messaging and social media. The solution consists of Stanley, a recruitment chatbot compatible with Facebook and WeChat, which conducts interviews and an application management tool, which allows recruiters to evaluate and hire candidates faster than ever before.
