Augmented Recruiter

Every month, we feature recruiters who embrace automation in our Augmented Recruiters Series.

This month we feature Ping Catalina from Alexander Mann Solutions.

5 Questions about Ping Sta. Catalina

Hello please tell us about your scope of responsibilities at Alexander Mann Solutions?

I am a Client Services Manager functioning in Recruiting for different BPO clients.

What do you like most working in recruitment?

It allows me to develop professional relationships where I learn best practices, be updated with interviewing techniques and latest technologies.

How has technology changed the way you work since you started out in recruitment?

It’s evolved in such a way that recruiting is no longer limited to an 8-hour job.

What new hack/tool/technology do you recommend to your fellow “augmented recruiters?

I have always recommended Talkpush because it is a non traditional tool that allows us access to several tools in one platform.

Enough about recruitment! What is your favorite science fiction movie of all time?

About Talkpush

Talkpush is making volume hiring fun and personal again. Talkpush is a recruitment platform that leverages the power of messaging and social media.
