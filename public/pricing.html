Telecom charges: Per completed interview

Talkpush charges a per-completed-interview fee for each completed interview at the end of the month. The charge varies depending on the method and the location of the call.

All inclusive package

If you would like to receive a quotation for an all-inclusive package, which would include all the telecom expenses (phone calls and SMS) that you project to be making, you can email hello@talkpush.com with an estimate for your total annual interviewing volumes (number of candidates interviewed) by country.

Interviews completed via the Web

Candidate can complete their digital interview over the web, either via an application they can download for free on their mobile phone, or via a compatible internet browser (Chrome, Firefox, Explorer)

  • Starter and Business Package: No telecom charges.
  • Enterprise Package: $0.50 per completed interview beyond the initial 7,000 completed interviews in the month.
