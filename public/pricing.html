Telecom charges: Per completed interview

Talkpush charges a per-completed-interview fee for each completed interview at the end of the month. The charge varies depending on the method and the location of the call.

All inclusive package

If you would like to receive a quotation for an all-inclusive package, which would include all the telecom expenses (phone calls and SMS) that you project to be making, you can email hello@talkpush.com with an estimate for your total annual interviewing volumes (number of candidates interviewed) by country.

Interviews completed via the Web / Messaging / Mobile App

Candidate can complete their digital interview over the web, via Facebook Messenger, via a mobile application (downloadable for free), or via a compatible internet browser (Chrome, Firefox, Explorer) on WebRTC. The initial 100 completed interviews completed via these methods will not be charged to you.

Beyond the initial 100 interviews completed over Web or Messaging or Mobile App, the cost per completed interview is $0.40.

Interviews completed over the phone

If you want to maximize your participation rate (some candidates may not want to use the mobile app or their browser), we recommend that you conduct the digital interviews by calling the candidates directly on their phone. It is still the most effective way to get a hold of someone.
