Bringing a commercial edge for RPO partners looking to turbocharge recruitment.


Talkpush is partnering with Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO) leaders, giving them an edge in converting online traffic into qualified leads and in getting their candidates heard by their clients ahead of other candidates.


 
 

Talkpush accelerates the recruitment operations for RPO in 3 fundamental ways:

 

Reach the people that matter most


  • By making it easy for candidate to qualify themselves 24/7 via a simple phone call, recruiters can widen the talent pool considerably –up to 10 times the number of qualified candidates generated with the same advertising budget
  • Because the interviews are conducted over the phone, your candidates don’t need to get in front of a computer or a webcam to complete them. Your recruiters can get all the top candidates to complete the interview, no matter where they are. Your recruiters stay in control of the whole process
  • With Talkpush, you can be sure that candidates will complete their initial phone screening within an hour of applying for the job 70% of the time. This means your recruiters can be the first to talk to the qualified candidates

Boost your team’s productivity


  • Your team can interview up to 6 times more qualified candidates thanks to the asynchronous interviewing
  • Reduce time to hire – reduce wasted scheduling and interview time with unqualified candidates
  • Automated sourcing and screening – reduce your time spent calling candidates
 

Increase your placement rates and reduce your time to hire


  • Give your clients a chance to hear your candidates, instead of just reading their resume and your interview notes. This will increase placement rates (by up to 50%) and reduce your time to hire (by up to 80%)
 
 
 

Hire better + Hire faster + Control the process end to end
= Better service / More time to win clients
